The journey

Watch the road to your license

New Jersey lays out a clear path from your first permit to a full license. Drive the car along the route, or tap a stop to see what happens at each milestone.

Step 01

Learner's permit

Pass your written knowledge and vision test at the MVC. You are 16 and ready. We help you study so the first try counts.

Step 02

Six hours behind the wheel

The required GDL training, done in our dual control car with a calm, certified instructor. This is where real confidence is built.

Step 03

The road test

Parallel parking, the K turn, smooth control. We rehearse the exact maneuvers so test day feels like just another lesson.

Step 04

Your full license

You pass, you drive, you are free. The keys are yours and the road is open. We will be cheering you on.
